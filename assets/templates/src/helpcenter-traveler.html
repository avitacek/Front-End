Technical Requirements

The following are the minimum and recommended technical requirements for using the TempoTrip website and application.

Supported Browser Versions

  • Chrome (newest three versions). Please note:
    • Use the default settings, which include cookies and JavaScript enabled.
    • See Chrome Help for instructions on how to change settings if you need to.
  • Safari (versions 6.0, 7.0, and 8.0). Please note:
    • Use the default settings, which include cookies and JavaScript enabled. 
    • See Apple Support for Safari for instructions on how to change settings if you need to.
  • Internet Explorer 11.0
  • Firefox (newest three versions). Please note:
    • Use the default settings, which include cookies and JavaScript enabled.
    • See Mozilla Support for instructions on how to change settings if you need to.

*Note: Cookies and JavaScript must be enabled in any of the browsers you use.

Warning:  Users should not open multiple tabs or windows of the browser for a single session when logged into your tempotrip acount and either in profile or searching for a flight. When multiple tabs or windows are open, actions in one tab or window can change the session, and the other tabs or windows may effect each other.

Traveler Profile

Your profile contains personal identification needed to book services. It also allows you to store preferences for services and communications. Using information in your profile reduces the time it takes you to arrange for services, because you don't have to enter them each time you purchase a flight.

To Update Profile

  1. Log In to your TempoTrip Account
  2. Click Profile on the left sidebar Navigation
  3. Complete all required fields and any other fields that you want.
  4. When you are finished, click Save Button located at bottom of the page .

Booking a Trip

Follow these instructions for searching and purchasing flight services as well as reviewing, canceling or modifying any booked flights.

Book a Flight

  1. Log In to your TempoTrip account
  2. Click Book Flights Icon  on the left sidebar Navigation
  3. In the flight search box form, enter your From and To airports / cities, select your Leave and Return dates and times.
  4. Click Search
    5. NOTE:

    For a One-way flight search click the "One Way" tab.  For a Round-trip flight search click the "Round Trip" tab. You can search for different Classes / Fare Types from the "Seat Class" drop down. To book for more than one person change "Traveler(s)" drop down to the desired amount of traveling adults.

  5. Sort all the displayed results by the lowest Total Fare, earliest Departure Time, earliest Arrival Time, least number of stops or the shortest duration by clicking the "Sorting By:" button.
  6. Filter your results by Stops, Airline, AM / PM Depart and Arrive times, or min and max price by using the left side filter bar.
  7. Click the "BOOK IT" button on the results flight card to choose that departure and return trip.
  8. Review your selected trip details and complete the Traveler Details and Billing Information forms below to purchase your ticket(s).
    9. NOTE:

    If the Billing Information Form is not shown that means your company or group administrator has added the payment method for you and you can finish booking your trip still by clicking submit payment button.

  9. Before purchasing, you must click the checkbox for the "* I hereby agree to the TempoTrip terms and conditions for this purchase." statement above the Submit Payment button. (You can click the terms and conditions link in the statement to see what they are.)
  10. Click the Submit Payment if you are ready to complete your booking reservation.

Canceling a Flight

To cancel a previously booked flight reservation, follow these steps:

  1. On the My Trips page, click on the upcoming trip that you want to cancel.
  2. Click the Cancel this trip button at the bottom of the right trip details column. The Cancel Trip Assistant page appears. 
  3. Click the checkbox for "I acknowledge and accept" to accept the terms for canceling the booking.
  4. Click Confirm Cancellation or Keep Original Trip.
  5. Click Done to finish. 

An email is sent to the traveler with the trip cancellation details. The status of the trip in the Upcoming Reservations section of the Home page is changed to “Cancelled”.

 

Changing a Flight

To make changes to a trip, follow the steps below:

  1. On the My Trips page, click on the upcoming trip that you want to change.

Note: If you used a single-use card for a purchase, the card information will be used for any changes. There is no need to reenter this information. If you add new services, you can select a card from your profile or the single-use card.

Viewing Booked Trips

To view previously booked trips, follow the steps below:

  1. While logged into your profile click on the  My Trips link on the left side navigation.
  2. On the My Trips page you will see a list of all your previously booked trips.
  3. Click on a trip to view more details or take action on that trip. More details will show up in the right hand column when clicked.
