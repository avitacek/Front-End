Getting Started TempoTrip Admin

Below you will find instructions on how to use TempoTrip

Accessing Your Admin Account

Activating Your Account

To activate your account:

  1. You should have recieved an email from TempoTrip Support. Click the "Get Started" link in the email.
  2. Create your account password in the form provided. You will be directed to your TempoTrip Profile dashboard
  3. Complete your profile details and click Submit to save.
  4. That's it! Now your admin account is now active.

Managing Travelers

As an administrator or support person with access to the Travelers tab at the master site level of the TempoTrip Dashboard, you can perform the basic functions of administering and managing the site's Travelers.

Below walks you through the TempoTrip Dashboard functions for adding travelers, Traveler activation, editing profiled travelers information, and booking for profiled travelers.

Add Individual Travelers

As an administrator, you can manually add a traveler to create a traveler profile.

  • The administrator adds the initial profile information, (First Name, Last Name, Email) and the traveler will be responsible to complete the rest of the profile information.
  • Required information to individually add a traveler:
    • Email: (Required) Enter the new traveler’s email address, which must be unique to the site.
    • First Name: (Required) Enter the new traveler’s first name. Note: It is important for the First, Middle and Last Name to agree with the government issued documents (such as a passport or driver's license) that will be presented at the airport for check in.
    • Last Name: (Required) Enter the new traveler’s last name.

To Add a Traveler

  1. Log In to your TempoTrip Account
  2. Click  Travelers on the left sidebar Navigation
  3. On the right hand side you will see the Add Traveler(s) area. Complete all required fields.
  4. When you are finished, click the  Add Traveler button

Bulk Upload Travelers

As an administrator, you can add multiple travelers to your site at once, with a profile upload file. To prepare a traveler profile upload, you need to prepare the data according to the upload file format for either a comma-separated values (CSV) file. If errors are found in the upload, the error can either prevent one traveler or the whole file of travelers from being uploaded, depending on the type of error.

You will need to use the following csv file as an upload template.

To Add Multiple Travelers

  1. Log In to your TempoTrip Account
  2. Click  Travelers on the left sidebar Navigation
  3. On the right hand side you will see the Add Traveler(s) area. At the bottom you will see a "Select a File"  button to upload your csv file with your list of travelers.
    You can also download the upload template HERE
  4. When you are finished, click the  Add Traveler button
NOTE:

When you add a traveler or when you upload multiple travelers at a time with csv, the process sends out an activation email with a log-in link to the traveler as an initial invitation.

Travelers will not be known to the system until the traveler clicks the link in email, creates a password and completes their traveler profile information.

Editing Travelers

As an Admin you can edit/modify an individual traveler’s profile information and their role in the TempoTrip Dashboard. Roles/Permissions can only be changed after they have been added as a traveler.

Edit a Traveler's Profile

  1. Log In to your TempoTrip Account
  2. Click  Travelers on the left sidebar Navigation
  3. Click on the traveler that you want to edit. Their profile detials will show on the right hand side.
  4. To edit, simply change any of the information already provided in the fields.
  5. When you are finished, click the  Save button

Booking For Other Traveler(s)

As a site administrator or arranger, you can book travel for another profiled traveler or multiple travelers if traveling together.

Follow these instructions for searching and purchasing flight services for multiple profiled travelers.

Book a Flight for Multiple Travelers

  1. Log In to your TempoTrip account
  2. Click Book Flights Icon  on the left sidebar Navigation
  3. In the flight search box form, enter your From and To airports / cities, select your Leave and Return dates and times.
  4. To book for more than one person change "Traveler(s)" drop down to the desired amount of traveling adults.
  5. Click Search
    6. NOTE:

    For a One-way flight search click the "One Way" tab.  For a Round-trip flight search click the "Round Trip" tab. You can search for different Classes / Fare Types from the "Seat Class" drop down.

  6. Sort all the displayed results by the lowest Total Fare, earliest Departure Time, earliest Arrival Time, least number of stops or the shortest duration by clicking the "Sorting By:" button.
  7. Filter your results by Stops, Airline, AM / PM Depart and Arrive times, or min and max price by using the left side filter bar.
  8. Click the "BOOK IT" button on the results flight card to choose that departure and return trip.
  9. Review your selected trip details and complete the Traveler Details and Billing Information forms below to purchase your ticket(s). There will be a traveler detail form for each traveler, depending on how many Traveler(s) you chose when you started your search.
  10. To add a profiled traveler, select from the dropdown provided at the top of each traveler detail form. Once selected the form will populate with all that travelers details.
    11. NOTE:

    If a profiled traveler is not shown in the dropdown it is because they have not yet activated their account and saved their profile information.

    If the Billing Information Form is not shown that means your company or group administrator has added the payment method for you and you can finish booking your trip still by clicking submit payment button.

  11. Before purchasing, you must click the checkbox for the "* I hereby agree to the TempoTrip terms and conditions for this purchase." statement above the Submit Payment button. (You can click the terms and conditions link in the statement to see what they are.)
  12. Click the Submit Payment if you are ready to complete your booking reservation.
